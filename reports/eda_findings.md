# Day 3 EDA Findings

1. Mutual fund NAVs show long-term growth from 2022–2026.
2. SBI Mutual Fund and HDFC Mutual Fund dominate AUM rankings.
3. SIP inflows exhibit a strong upward trend.
4. Equity-oriented categories attract the highest net inflows.
5. Investor participation is concentrated in key Indian states.
6. The 26–35 age group contributes the largest share of transactions.
7. Industry folio counts have steadily increased.
8. Large-cap funds show lower volatility than small-cap funds.
9. Banking and Financial sectors represent a major portion of portfolio holdings.
10. Most equity funds are positively correlated with market movements.
